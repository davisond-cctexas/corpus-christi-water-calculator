Residential Water and Wastewater Charges Calculator

Estimate water and wastewater monthly charges based on the amount of gallons you expect to consume for the month and the meter size.

Disclaimer: This online calculator is for estimating the residential water and wastewater charges only, based on a 30‐day billing cycle. Solid Waste, Gas and Streets charges are not included. Your actual residential water and wastewater charges may vary depending on any rate changes and the length of the billing cycle. For rate information visit http://www.cctexas.com/services/utilities/water-rates-and-charges. For questions or comments, please email us at _________________@cctexas.com. or call (361) 826-CITY(2489).

Instructions: Provide the information requested below. Your water usage can be found on your utility invoice. Some accounts have more than one meter. For those accounts with multiple meters, you will need to run the calculator for each meter. There are no wastewater charges for an irrigation meter.

Example: If you want to estimate the cost of 8,000 gallons of water, enter 8 in the designated space; if you want to estimate 10,000 gallons, enter 10. The most common residential meter size is 5/8". Your residential meter size is going to be 5/8” or 1”. If you don’t know what meter size you have at home, call (361) 826-CITY(2489) to find out.
